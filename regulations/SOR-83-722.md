# Summary
EXPORT AND IMPORT PERMITS ACT General Export Permit No. Ex.
The Secretary of State for External Affairs, pursuant to section 6 of the  Export Permit Regulations , C.R.C., c.
602, hereby issues the annexed  General Export Permit No. Ex.
Ottawa, September 23, 1983 ALLAN JOSEPH M ac EACHEN Secretary of State for External Affairs Short Title.
This Permit may be cited as the  Export of Sugar Permit .
General.
Any person may, under the authority of this General Export Permit, export from Canada to the United States any goods described in item 5201 of the  Export Control List  on condition that the goods to be exported are for the personal use of the exporter and the exporter’s household; and each exportation of the goods does not exceed 5 kg in weight.
1. Where completion and validation of customs entry form B-13 is required in respect of any goods that are exported under the authority of this General Export Permit, the form shall be endorsed “Exported under the authority of General Export Permit No. Ex.
10” or “Exporté en vertu de la Licence générale d’exportation n o  Ex. 10”.
