# Summary
ATLANTIC FISHERIES RESTRUCTURING ACT Order Authorizing the Minister of State (Privatization and Regulatory Affairs) to Exercise the Powers Given to the Minister of Fisheries and Oceans Under the Act in Respect of Certain Companies.
1987-256 Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Prime Minister, pursuant to section 7 of the  Atlantic Fisheries Restructuring Act * , is pleased hereby S.C. 1980-81-82-83, c.
172 to revoke Order in Council P.C. 1985-2003 of June 20, 1985 ** ; and SOR/85-594, 1985  Canada Gazette  Part II, p.
2961 to authorize the Minister of State (Privatization and Regulatory Affairs) to exercise the powers given to the Minister of Fisheries and Oceans pursuant to the  Atlantic Fisheries Restructuring Act  in respect of 125459 Canada Limited, Fishery Products International Limited and 125457 Canada Limited known as NSHOLDCO Limited.
