# Summary
NATIONAL ENERGY BOARD ACT Order Respecting the Application of Part IV of the National Energy Board Act. Short Title.
This Order may be cited as the  National Energy Board Order No. MO-62-69 .
Application of part IV of the act.
The National Energy Board, pursuant to subsection 97(1) 1  of the  National Energy Board Act , hereby orders that Part IV of the said Act applies to every person who was operating a pipeline on November 2, 1959.
This subsection is not reproduced in the R.S.C., 1970, see S.C., 1959, c.
