# Summary
DOMINION SUCCESSION DUTY ACT Regulations Respecting Section 12 of the Dominion Succession Duty Act. 1957-814 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 12 of the  Dominion Succession Duty Act , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Regulations Respecting section 12 of the Dominion Succession Duty Act .
These Regulations may be cited as the  Dominion Succession Duty (1957) Regulations .
The Province of Ontario and the Province of Quebec are hereby prescribed for the purposes of section 12 of the  Dominion Succession Duty Act .
