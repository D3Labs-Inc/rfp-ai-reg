# Summary
CUSTOMS TARIFF Regulations Prescribing Agricultural Goods in Respect of Any Country for the Purposes of Imposing a Safeguard Surtax, 1995-2.
1995-1278  His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 60.01(7) *  of the  Customs Tariff ** , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Regulations prescribing agricultural goods in respect of any country for the purpose of imposing a safeguard surtax, 1995-2, effective August 1, 1995 .
41 (3rd Supp.) Short Title.
These Regulations may be cited as the  Safeguard Surtax Regulations, 1995-2 .
Goods Subject to Safeguard Surtax.
For the purposes of section 68 of the  Customs Tariff , the following agricultural goods, classified under the tariff item numbers that follow them, are prescribed in respect of any country: cream (0401.30.10, 0401.30.20); powdered whey (0404.10.21, 0404.10.22); and butter and other fats and oils derived from milk (0405.10.10, 0405.10.20, 0405.90.10, 0405.90.20).
