# Summary
AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS MARKETING ACT Saskatchewan Flax Order.
2001-1640 Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food, pursuant to section 2 a of the  Agricultural Products Marketing Act , hereby makes the annexed  Saskatchewan Flax Order .
Act  means  The Agri-Food Act , Statutes of Saskatchewan, S.S. 1990-91, c.
( Loi ) Commission  means the Saskatchewan Flax Development Commission established pursuant to the Act.
( lin ) Plan  means The Saskatchewan Flax Development Plan, established pursuant to section 3 of  The Saskatchewan Flax Development Plan Regulations , R.R.S. c.
The Commission is authorized to regulate the marketing of flax in interprovincial and export trade and for those purposes may, with respect to persons and property situated in Saskatchewan, exercise all or any powers like the powers exercisable by the Commission under the Act and the Plan in relation to the marketing of flax locally, within Saskatchewan.
This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
