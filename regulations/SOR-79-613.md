# Summary
STATUTORY INSTRUMENTS ACT Regulations Respecting a Special Issue of Part II of the Canada Gazette To Be Published in Conjunction with the Consolidated Regulations of Canada, 1978.
1979-2183 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Justice, pursuant to subsection 13(2) and paragraph 27(m) of the  Statutory Instruments Act , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Regulations respecting a special issue of Part II of the Canada Gazette to be published in conjunction with the Consolidated Regulations of Canada, 1978 .
In these Regulations,  special issue  means the issue of Part II of the  Canada Gazette  to be published in conjunction with the  Consolidated Regulations of Canada, 1978 , on the order of the Clerk of the Privy Council, made pursuant to section 13 of the  Statutory Instruments Regulations .
Notwithstanding section 19 of the  Statutory Instruments Regulations , a copy of each volume of the special issue may be sold to any person on payment of the sum of $34.50.
