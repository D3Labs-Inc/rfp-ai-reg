# Summary
FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT Order Respecting the Remission of Excise Duty on Spirits Destroyed by Accident.
This Order may be cited as the  Spirit Destruction Remission Order .
In this Order, Act  means the  Excise Act ; ( Loi ) bonding warehouse  has the same meaning as assigned to that term by the Act.
( entrep√¥t ) Remission.
Subject to section 4, remission of the excise duty imposed pursuant to subsection 1(1) of Part I of the schedule to the Act and payable pursuant to section 52 and subsection 54(1) of the Act is hereby granted where spirits are destroyed due to accidents incurred when transporting the spirits in bond; or while handling the spirits in licensed bonding warehouses.
Conditions.
Remission under section 3 may be granted on the condition that a request therefor in writing is forwarded to the Minister of National Revenue together with documentary evidence that establishes to his satisfaction that the spirits have not been illegally removed from the bonding warehouse; were not destroyed through the failure of the shipper, warehouse operator or transportation company to exercise reasonable diligence and effective control of his operations; and were destroyed before being released from the control of excise officers.
