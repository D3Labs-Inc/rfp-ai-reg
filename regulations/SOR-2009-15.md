# Summary
PERFLUOROOCTANE SULFONATE VIRTUAL ELIMINATION ACT Regulations Adding Perfluorooctane Sulfonate and Its Salts to the Virtual Elimination List.
The Minister of the Environment and the Minister of Health, pursuant to subsection 3(1) of the  Perfluorooctane Sulfonate Virtual Elimination Act a , hereby make the annexed  Regulations Adding Perfluorooctane Sulfonate and Its Salts to the Virtual Elimination List .
13 Ottawa, December 7, 2008 JIM PRENTICE Minister of the Environment Ottawa, January 9, 2009 LEONA AGLUKKAQ Minister of Health Addition.
Perfluorooctane sulfonate and its salts are added to column 1 of the  Virtual Elimination List 1 , compiled under subsection 65(2) of the  Canadian Environmental Protection Act, 1999 2 .
SOR/2006-298 S.C. 1999, c.
33 Coming into Force.
These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.
