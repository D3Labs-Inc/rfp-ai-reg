# Summary
PUBLIC SERVICE EMPLOYMENT ACT Regulations Respecting the Appointment of Persons to the Communications Security Establishment of the Department of National Defence.
Short Title.
These Regulations may be cited as the  Communications Security Establishment Appointments Regulations .
Appointments.
The Deputy Minister of National Defence may appoint such persons as he deems proper to the Communications Security Establishment of the Department of National Defence.
