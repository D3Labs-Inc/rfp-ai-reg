# Summary
CUSTOMS TARIFF CHFTA Tariff Preference Regulations.
2014-981 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 16(2) a  of the  Customs Tariff b , makes the annexed  CHFTA Tariff Preference Regulations .
In these Regulations,  originating  means qualifying as originating in the territory of a Party under the rules of origin set out in Chapter Four (Rules of Origin) of the Canada–Honduras Free Trade Agreement.
General.
Coming into Force.
* 3 These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 43 of the  Canada–Honduras Economic Growth and Prosperity Act , chapter 14 of the Statutes of Canada, 2014, comes into force, but if these Regulations are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.
[Note: Regulations in force October 1, 2014,  see  SI/2014-74.]
