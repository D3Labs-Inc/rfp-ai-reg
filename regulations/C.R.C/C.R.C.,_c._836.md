# Summary
FISHERIES ACT Notice Excepting Dogfish from the Operation of Section 29 of the Fisheries Act. Short Title.
This Notice may be cited as the  Dogfish Exemption Notice .
Exemption.
Dogfish and other fish caught incidental to fishing for dogfish in the Goose Island Banks of Hecate Straits are hereby excepted from the operation of section 29 of the  Fisheries Act .
