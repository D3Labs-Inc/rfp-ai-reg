# Summary
PUBLIC SERVICE PENSION ADJUSTMENT ACT Regulations Made Pursuant to the Public Service Pension Adjustment Act. Short Title.
These Regulations may be cited as the  Public Service Pension Adjustment Regulations .
Interpretation.
In these Regulations, Act  means the  Public Service Pension Adjustment Act ; ( Loi ) re-employed recipient  means a recipient who holds any office or position or performs any services, the remuneration for which is payable out of the Consolidated Revenue Fund or by an agent of Her Majesty in right of Canada.
The increase in the pension payable to a re-employed recipient pursuant to the Act shall be suspended where, under the enactment or pension plan pursuant to which the pension is payable, the pension of the recipient is suspended in whole or in part.
Where, under the enactment or pension plan pursuant to which the pension of a re-employed recipient is payable, no part of the pension of the recipient is suspended, the increase in the pension payable to the recipient pursuant to the Act shall be paid to the extent that it would be payable if the increase were part of the pension payable to the recipient under the said enactment or pension plan.
