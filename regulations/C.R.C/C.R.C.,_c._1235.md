# Summary
NORTHWEST TERRITORIES ACT Order Designating Additional Legislative Powers Vested in the Commissioner in Council.
Short Title.
This Order may be cited as the  Additional Legislative Powers Designation Order .
Designation.
The making of grants and loans to individuals and the making of loans to persons are hereby designated as matters in relation to which the Commissioner in Council of the Northwest Territories may make Ordinances.
