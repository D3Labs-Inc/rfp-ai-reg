# Summary
FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT Order Respecting the Remission of Customs Duty and Sales Tax on Computer Generated Mailing Lists.
Short Title.
This Order may be cited as the  Computer Generated Mailing List Remission Order .
Remission.
Remission is hereby granted of the customs duty paid or payable under Schedule A to the  Customs Tariff  on computer generated mailing lists imported during the period commencing on February 14, 1972 and ending on June 30, 1985.
1. Remission is hereby granted of the sales tax paid or payable under the  Excise Tax Act  on the goods referred to in section 2 in the amount equal to the difference between the sales tax calculated on the duty-paid value of the goods and the sales tax calculated on the value for duty of the goods.
