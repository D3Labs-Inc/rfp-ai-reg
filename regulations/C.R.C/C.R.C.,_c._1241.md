# Summary
NUCLEAR LIABILITY ACT Order Designating the Minister for the Purposes of the Nuclear Liability Act. Short Title.
This Order may be cited as the  Minister Designation Order (Nuclear Liability Act) .
Designation.
The Minister of Energy, Mines and Resources is hereby designated as the member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada to act as the Minister for the purposes of the  Nuclear Liability Act .
