# Summary
CUSTOMS ACT EXCISE ACT Regulations Respecting Special Customs and Excise Services.
Every person for whom a special service is provided shall pay in respect of that service a charge of for the first 2 hours or portion thereof   $54; and for each hour or portion thereof in excess of 2 hours   $27; and provide or pay for the transportation and living expenses of, and suitable accommodation for, each officer required to render such service.
1. The collector of customs and excise at a port from which a special service is requested may require the person requesting the service to deposit security for payment of the charges for that service.
The security referred to in subsection (1) shall be in the form of cash, a certified cheque, Government of Canada bonds or a general bond entered into with Her Majesty, in an amount prescribed by the collector, which amount shall not exceed the estimated charges of the service.
