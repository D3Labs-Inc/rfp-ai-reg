# Summary
CANADA POST CORPORATION ACT Regulations Respecting the Reproduction of Postage Stamps.
Reproductions Authorized.
Any reproduction made and published pursuant to section 2 shall be at least 50 per cent larger or 50 per cent smaller, by area measurement, than the postage stamp of which it is a reproduction; or clearly defaced by means of a line that is embodied in the plate or die from which the reproduction is made.
Plates or dies used or employed in the reproduction of postage stamps pursuant to section 2 shall be made entirely of babbit or other soft metal or material.
Reproduction with Consent of the Canada Post Corporation.
In addition to reproductions of postage stamps authorized by section 2, reproduction of postage stamps may, where the Canada Post Corporation considers that it will be in the public interest, be made or published with the written consent of the Canada Post Corporation, which consent may be sought by an application in writing addressed to the following address: DIRECTOR OF STAMP PRODUCTS CANADA POST 2701 RIVERSIDE DR OTTAWA, ONTARIO K1A 0B1 SOR/81-845, s.
