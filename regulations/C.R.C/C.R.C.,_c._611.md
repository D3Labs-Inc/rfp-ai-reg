# Summary
EXPORT AND IMPORT PERMITS ACT General Export Permit No. Ex.
This Permit may be cited as the  Export of Goods for Special and Personal Use Permit .
Subject to section 4, any person may, under the authority of this General Export Permit, export from Canada to any country any goods that have a value of $100 or less.
Subsection (1) does not authorize the exportation of goods that are separated into units the aggregate value of which exceeds $100.
