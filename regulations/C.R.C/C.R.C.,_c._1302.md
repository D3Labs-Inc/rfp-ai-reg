# Summary
PRECIOUS METALS MARKING ACT Order Designating the Minister for the Purposes of the Precious Metals Marking Act. Short Title.
This Order may be cited as the  Minister Designation Order (Precious Metals Marking Act) .
Designation.
The Minister of Consumer and Corporate Affairs, a member of the Queen’s Privy Council for Canada, is hereby designated as the Minister for the purposes of the  Precious Metals Marking Act .
