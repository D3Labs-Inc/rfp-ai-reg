# Summary
CUSTOMS TARIFF Order Respecting the Remission of Customs Duties on Billiard Cloth for the Manufacture or Repair of Billiard Tables.
1996-496 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 101 of the  Customs Tariff * , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Order respecting the remission of customs duties on billiard cloth for the manufacture or repair of billiard tables .
41 (3rd Supp.) Short Title.
This Order may be cited as the  Billiard Cloth Remission Order, 1996 .
Remission.
Subject to section 3, remission is hereby granted of the customs duties paid or payable under Schedule I to the  Customs Tariff  on billiard cloth, of tariff item Nos. 5111.19.00 or 5112.19.10, for the manufacture or repair of billiard tables.
Conditions.
The remission is granted pursuant to section 2 on the condition that: the billiard cloth is imported into Canada during the period commencing on July 1, 1995 and ending on December 31, 1997; and a claim for remission is made to the Minister of National Revenue within three years of the date of importation of the goods for which remission is claimed.
