# Summary
CUSTOMS TARIFF CETA Rules of Origin Regulations.
2017-1124 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 16(2) a  of the  Customs Tariff b , makes the annexed  CETA Rules of Origin Regulations .
36 Rules of Origin.
The following provisions of the Protocol on Rules of Origin and Origin Procedures of the Canada–European Union Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement have the force of law in Canada: Articles 1 and 2; paragraphs 1 to 3 of Article 3; Articles 4 to 17; and Annexes 1, 4, 5, 5‑A and 7.
Coming into Force.
* 2 These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 97 of the  Canada–European Union Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement Implementation Act , chapter 6 of the Statutes of Canada, 2017, comes into force, but if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.
[Note: Regulations in force September 21, 2017,  see  SI/2017-47.]
