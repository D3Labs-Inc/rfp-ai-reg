# Summary
EMPLOYMENT EQUITY ACT Order Specifying the Royal Canadian Mounted Police for the Purposes of the Employment Equity Act. 2002-1958 Whereas, pursuant to paragraph 4(1)(d) of the  Employment Equity Act a , the Treasury Board has consulted the Solicitor General of Canada concerning the application of that Act to the Royal Canadian Mounted Police; S.C. 1995, c.
44 Therefore, Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Labour and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 4(1)(d) of the  Employment Equity Act a , hereby makes the annexed  Order Specifying the Royal Canadian Mounted Police for the Purposes of the Employment Equity Act .
Designation.
The Royal Canadian Mounted Police is specified under paragraph 4(1)(d) of the  Employment Equity Act  as a portion of the public sector employing one hundred or more employees to which that Act applies.
Coming into Force.
This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
