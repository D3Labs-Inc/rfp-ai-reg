# Title
Canada Communication Group Divestiture Regulations


# ID
SOR-97-127

# URL
http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-97-127/FullText.html


# Summary
Subject to subsection (2), these Regulations apply to a person who, having received and accepted an offer of employment from St. Joseph Corporation pursuant to the sales agreement, on the transfer date ceases to be employed in the public service and becomes employed by the new employer.
For the purposes of subsection 13(3) of the Act, the surviving spouse and children of a person who dies while employed by the new employer are entitled to an allowance as described in paragraphs 12(4)(a) and (b) of the Act, subject to the limitations set out in subsections 12(4) and (5) of the Act. For the purposes of subsection 26(2) of the Act, a child who was born to or adopted by a person or became the stepchild of the person during the period that begins on the date on which the person ceases to be employed in the public service and that ends on the date on which that person ceases to be employed by the new employer is entitled to an allowance under Part I of the Act.
