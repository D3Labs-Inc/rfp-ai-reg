# Title
Fort Frances Airport Zoning Regulations


# ID
SOR-96-133

# URL
http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-96-133/FullText.html


# Summary
Where an object of natural growth that is on any land to which these Regulations apply grows to a height that exceeds in elevation at the location of the object any of the surfaces referred to in section 4, the Minister of Transport may require that the owner or lessee of the land remove the excess growth.
The strips, shown on Fort Frances Airport Zoning Plan No. 59-011 91-60, dated January 14, 1993, are described as follows: the strip associated with runway 12-30 is 150 m in width, 75 m being on each side of the centre line of the runway, and 1 488.9 m in length; and the strip associated with runway 03-21 is 60 m in width, 30 m being on each side of the centre line of the runway, and 860.05 m in length.
