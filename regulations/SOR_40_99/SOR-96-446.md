# Summary
MOTOR VEHICLE SAFETY ACT Nova BUS Corporation Exemption Order.
1996-1455 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Transport, pursuant to paragraph 9(1)(c) of the  Motor Vehicle Safety Act a , hereby makes the annexed  Nova BUS Corporation Exemption Order , being of the opinion that conformity with certain prescribed standards would impede the development of new kinds of buses.
16 Exemption.
Six hundred and fifty units of Nova LFS low-floor model buses, manufactured by Nova BUS Corporation, are exempt from the requirements in subsections 217(11), (14), (17) and (19) of Schedule IV to the  Motor Vehicle Safety Regulations  that are applicable to push-out windows, for a period of two years beginning on the day on which this Order is registered under the  Statutory Instruments Act .
Coming into Force.
This Order comes into force on September 17, 1996.
