# Summary
CURRENCY ACT Regulations Respecting Conversion of Values Expressed in Gold Francs into Canadian Dollar Equivalents for Purposes of the Canada Shipping Act. 1978-104 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance and the Minister of Transport, is pleased hereby to revoke, pursuant to subsection 651(1) of the  Canada Shipping Act , the Gold Franc Equivalent Order made by Order in Council P.C. 1975-2579 of 28th October, 1975 1 , as amended 2 ; and to make, pursuant to section 2 of  An Act to amend the Currency and Exchange Act and other Acts in consequence thereof , being chapter 38 of the Statutes of Canada, 1976-77, the annexed  Regulations respecting Conversion of Values expressed in Gold Francs into Canadian Dollar Equivalents for purposes of the Canada Shipping Act .
For the purposes of the  Canada Shipping Act , the equivalent dollar value of gold francs shall be determined as follows: gold francs shall be converted into S.D.R.’s at the exchange rate of 15.075 gold francs per S.D.R.; and S.D.R.’s shall be converted into Canadian dollars at the exchange rate established by the International Monetary Fund for S.D.R.’s and Canadian dollars.
