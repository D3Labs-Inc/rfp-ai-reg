# Summary
COOPERATIVE CREDIT ASSOCIATIONS ACT Regulations Prescribing the Maximum Fee that an Assocition May Charge for a Security Certificate Issued in Respect of a Transfer.
1992-912 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 92(2) and section 463 of the  Cooperative Credit Associations Act * , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Regulations prescribing the maximum fee that an association may charge for a security certificate issued in respect of a transfer , effective June 1, 1992.
48 Short Title.
These Regulations may be cited as the  Security Certificate Transfer Fee (Cooperative Credit Associations) Regulations .
Maximum Fee. An association may charge a fee not exceeding five dollars for a security certificate issued in respect of a transfer.
