# Summary
DIVORCE ACT Order Designating the Province of Manitoba for the Purposes of the Definition “applicable guidelines” in subsection 2(1) of the Divorce Act. 1998-835  Whereas the Province of Manitoba has established comprehensive guidelines for the determination of child support that deal with the matters referred to in section 26.1 a  of the  Divorce Act b ; S.C. 1997, c.
3 (2nd Supp.) Therefore, His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Justice, pursuant to subsection 2(5) c  of the  Divorce Act b , hereby makes the annexed  Order Designating the Province of Manitoba for the Purposes of the Definition “applicable guidelines” in subsection 2(1) of the Divorce Act .
1(4) Designation.
The Province of Manitoba is hereby designated for the purposes of the definition  applicable guidelines  in subsection 2(1) of the  Divorce Act .
Guidelines.
For the purposes of subsection 2(5) of the  Divorce Act , the law that constitutes the comprehensive guidelines for the Province of Manitoba is the  Child Support Guidelines Regulation , Man. Reg.
Coming into Force.
This Order comes into force on June 1, 1998.
