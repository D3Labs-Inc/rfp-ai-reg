# Summary
CUSTOMS TARIFF Time Limit for the Application of Subsection 118(1) of the Customs Tariff Regulations.
1997-2031  His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to paragraph 118(4)(a) of the  Customs Tariff a , hereby makes the annexed  Time Limit for the Application of Subsection 118(1) of the Customs Tariff Regulations .
36 Time Limit for Application.
Subsection 118(1) of the  Customs Tariff  applies in respect of any imported goods for a period of six years after the day on which the goods are released.
Coming into Force.
These Regulations come into force on January 1, 1998.
