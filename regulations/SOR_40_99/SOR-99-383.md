# Summary
CANADA DEPOSIT INSURANCE CORPORATION ACT Exemption from Deposit Insurance By-law (Interest on Deposits).
The Board of Directors of the Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation, pursuant to paragraph 11(2)(g) a  and subparagraph 26.03(1)(d)(ii) b  of the  Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation Act , hereby makes the annexed  Exemption from Deposit Insurance By-law (Interest on Deposits) .
18 (3rd Supp.), s.
For the purpose of subparagraph 26.03(1)(d)(ii) of the  Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation Act , the interest payable in relation to a deposit shall be determined in the following manner: if the deposit contract specifies that the interest payable is to be determined by reference to an index or reference point referred to in subsection 14(2.51) of the Act, in accordance with the  Interest Payable on Certain Deposits By-law , except that the interest termination date referred to in that By-law is deemed to be the date on which payment is made under subparagraph 26.03(1)(d)(ii) of the Act; and in any other case, in accordance with the provisions of the deposit contract, except that the maturity date of the deposit is deemed to be the date on which payment is made under that subparagraph.
Coming into Force.
This By-law comes into force on October 15, 1999.
