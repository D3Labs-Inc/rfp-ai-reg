# Summary
FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT Capacity Plates and Conformity Plates Charges Order.
The Minister of Transport, pursuant to Order in Council P.C. 1994-642 of April 21, 1994 a , made pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(b) b  of the  Financial Administration Act , hereby makes the annexed  Capacity Plates and Conformity Plates Charges Order .
6 November 26, 1998 David Collenette Minister of the Transport Interpretation.
The definitions in this section apply in this Order.
capacity plate  means a plate pertaining to the recommended safe limits of engine power and recommended gross load capacity.
( plaque de capacité ) conformity plate  means a notice in the form of a plate that pertains to compliance with construction standards.
( plaque de conformité ) Charges.
The charge to be paid for the issue of a capacity plate is $5.
The charge to be paid for the issue of a conformity plate is $5.
Coming into Force.
This Order comes into force on April 1, 1999.
