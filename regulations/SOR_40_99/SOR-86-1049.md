# Summary
ENERGY ADMINISTRATION ACT Order Declaring Sections 53 to 65 of the Energy Administration Act To Be Non-Applicable in Respect of any Producer-Province.
1986-2427 Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Energy, Mines and Resources, pursuant to subsection 52(1.1) *  of the  Energy Administration Act ** , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Order declaring sections 53 to 65 of the Energy Administration Act to be non-applicable in respect of any producer-province , effective November 1, 1986.
2 Short Title.
This Order may be cited as the  Energy Administration Act Sections 53 to 65 Non-application Order, 1986 .
Declaration.
Sections 53 to 65 of the  Energy Administration Act  do not apply in respect of any producer-province.
