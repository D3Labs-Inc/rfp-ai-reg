# Summary
AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS MARKETING ACT Alberta Chicken Order.
1999-524 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food, pursuant to section 2 a  of the  Agricultural Products Marketing Act , hereby makes the annexed  Alberta Chicken Order .
Act  means  The Marketing of Agricultural Products Act  of Alberta.
( poulet ) Commodity Board  means the Alberta Chicken Producers Board established under the Act and the Plan.
( Office de commercialisation ) Plan  means the  Alberta Chicken Producers Plan Regulations  made under the Act.
The Commodity Board is authorized to regulate the marketing of chickens in interprovincial and export trade, and for that purpose may, with respect to persons and property situated within the Province of Alberta, exercise all or any powers like the powers exercisable by it in relation to the marketing of chickens locally within that Province under the Act and the Plan.
[Repeal] Coming into Force.
This Order comes into force on March 25, 1999.
