# Summary
EXCISE TAX ACT Regulations Prescribing Purposes for Which New Successor Corporations Shall Be Deemed to Be the Same Corporations as and Continuations of Predecessor Amalgamated or Wound-Up Corporations.
1990-2742  His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 277(1) *  of the  Excise Tax Act , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Regulations prescribing purposes for which new successor corporations shall be deemed to be the same corporations as and continuations of predecessor amalgamated or wound-up corporations .
These Regulations may be cited as the  Amalgamations and Windings-Up Continuation (GST/HST) Regulations .
