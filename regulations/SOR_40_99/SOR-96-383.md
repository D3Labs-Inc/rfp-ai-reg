# Summary
CANADA TRANSPORTATION ACT Discontinuance and Continuance of Proceedings Order, 1996.
Proceedings in respect of the following classes of matters shall be continued in accordance with the provisions of the  Railway Act  as it read immediately before July 1, 1996: an application or complaint under section 212 of the  Railway Act  in respect of drainage; an approval under subsection 213(4) of the  Railway Act  of the character of works, specifications or plans in respect of drainage works; an application for a farm crossing under section 216 of the  Railway Act ; and a proceeding under section 230 of the  Railway Act .
This Order comes into force on July 10, 1996.
