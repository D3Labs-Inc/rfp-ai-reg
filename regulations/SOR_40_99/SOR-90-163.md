# Summary
FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT Order Authorizing Nordion International Inc. to Incorporate Five Corporations under the Laws of the Netherlands, France, the United Kingdom, Italy and the Federal Republic of Germany.
1990-434  Whereas the Governor in Council, pursuant to subsection 91(6) of the  Financial Administration Act , is satisfied that Nordion International Inc., as a corporation incorporated under the  Canada Business Corporations Act , is empowered to undertake the transaction described in the annexed Order; Therefore, His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of State (Privatization and Regulatory Affairs) and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 91(1)(a) of the  Financial Administration Act , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Order authorizing Nordion International Inc. to incorporate five corporations under the laws of the Netherlands, France, the United Kingdom, Italy and the Federal Republic of Germany .
This Order may be cited as the  Nordion International Incorporation Authorization Order  (Incorporation of Corporations).
