# Summary
CUSTOMS TARIFF Regulations prescribing a territory for the purposes of the definition “country” in the Customs Tariff.
1996-2073  His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 2(3) a  of the  Customs Tariff b , hereby makes the annexed  Regulations prescribing a territory for the purposes of the definition  .
country  in the Customs Tariff S.C. 1996, c.
41 (3rd Supp.) Prescription.
The territory where the customs laws of Israel are applied in accordance with Article III of the Protocol on Economic Relations set out in Annex V to the Israeli-Palestinian Interim Agreement on the West Bank and the Gaza Strip, dated September 28, 1995, as that Protocol is amended from time to time, is a prescribed territory for the purposes of the definition  country  in subsection 2(1) of the  Customs Tariff .
Coming into Force.
* 2 These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 41 of the  Canada-Israel Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act , being chapter 33 of the Statutes of Canada, 1996, comes into force.
[Note: Regulations in force January 1, 1997,  see  SI/97-9.]
