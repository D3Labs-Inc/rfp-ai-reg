# Title

# ID

# URL

# Summary
FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT Regulations Prescribing the Fees To Be Paid by the Users of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System.
1994-1340  His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Solicitor General of Canada and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(a) *  of the  Financial Administration Act , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Regulations prescribing the fees to be paid by the users of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System .
6 Short Title.
These Regulations may be cited as the  Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System Fees Regulations .
Fee. The fee to be paid by law enforcement agencies for the use of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Automated Fingerprint Identification System is $33.79 annually per sworn police officer.
