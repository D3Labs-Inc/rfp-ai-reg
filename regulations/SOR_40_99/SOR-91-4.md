# Summary
HEALTH OF ANIMALS ACT Regulations Excluding Certain Buildings and Premises from the Definition “Hatchery”.
The Minister of Agriculture, pursuant to subsection 2(2) of the  Health of Animals Act * , hereby makes the annexed  Regulations excluding certain buildings and premises from the definition “hatchery” .
21 Dated at Ottawa, the 10th day of December, 1990 DON MAZANKOWSKI Minister of Agriculture Short Title.
These Regulations may be cited as the  Hatchery Exclusion Regulations .
Exclusions.
The following buildings and premises are excluded from the definition  hatchery : any buildings or premises that have an incubator capacity of less than 1,000 eggs; and any buildings or premises that are not used to store for incubation chicken, turkey, duck, goose or game bird eggs.
