# Summary
CUSTOMS TARIFF Order Respecting the Exemption of Goods that Originate in the People’s Republic of China from the Direct Shipment Condition.
1985-360 Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 3.1(1.1) *  of the  Customs Tariff , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Order respecting the exemption of goods that are the product of the People’s Republic of China from the direct shipment condition , effective March 1, 1985.
1 Short Title.
This Order may be cited as the  China Direct Shipment Condition Exemption Order .
Subject to section 3, an exemption is hereby granted from the condition of direct shipment in paragraph 40(b) of the  Customs Tariff  with respect to goods that originate in the People’s Republic of China.
SOR/88-76.
Condition.
Exemption pursuant to section 2 is granted on condition that the goods are imported into Canada and released and accounted for under the  Customs Act  after January 1, 1988; the goods were shipped through Hong Kong on a through bill of lading to a consignee in Canada; and the importer submits to the Minister of National Revenue any documentation requested by the Minister relating to the exemption.
