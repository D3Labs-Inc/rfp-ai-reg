# Summary
EXCISE TAX ACT Regulations Respecting the Disclosure of Social Insurance Numbers for the Purpose of Registration under Section 240 of the Excise Tax Act. 1990-2751  His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to paragraph 277(1)(d) *  of the  Excise Tax Act , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Regulations respecting the disclosure of Social Insurance Numbers for the purpose of registration under section 240 of the Excise Tax Act .
12(1) Short Title.
These Regulations may be cited as the  Social Insurance Number Disclosure Regulations .
Disclosure Requirement.
Every individual who applies to the Minister to be registered pursuant to section 240 of the  Excise Tax Act  in the form prescribed by the Minister for that purpose shall disclose therein the individual’s Social Insurance Number.
