# Summary
COASTING TRADE ACT Regulations Specifying Territories and Indicating International Registers.
2017-1119 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Transport, pursuant to paragraphs 7(a) a  and (b) a  of the  Coasting Trade Act b , makes the annexed  Regulations Specifying Territories and Indicating International Registers .
31 Territories in Schedule 1 The territories set out in Schedule 1 are not referred to in the definition  territory of the European Union  in subsection 2(1) of the  Coasting Trade Act .
International registers The registers set out in Schedule 2 are international registers for the purpose of paragraph 3(2.1)(c) of the  Coasting Trade Act .
Coming into force * 3 These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 94 of the  Canada–European Union Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement Implementation Act , chapter 6 of the Statutes of Canada, 2017, comes into force but, if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.
