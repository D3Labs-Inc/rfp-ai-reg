# Summary
SOFTWOOD LUMBER PRODUCTS EXPORT CHARGE ACT, 2006 Expiry of the Application of Section 12.1 of the Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006 Regulations.
2011-732 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to section 102 of the  Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006 a  hereby makes the annexed  Expiry of the Application of Section 12.1 of the Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006 Regulations .
13 Section 12.1 of the  Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006  ceases to be in force on July 1, 2011.
These Regulations come into force on July 1, 2011.
