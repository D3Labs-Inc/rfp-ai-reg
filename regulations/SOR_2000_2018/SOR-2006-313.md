# Title
Insider Reports (Trust and Loan Companies) Regulations


# ID
SOR-2006-313

# URL
http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-2006-313/FullText.html


# Summary
For the purpose of paragraph 273(a) of the Act, an insider means an insider as it is defined, for the purpose of insider reports, under any legislation set out in column 2 of the schedule.
For the purpose of paragraph 273(b) of the Act, the required form and content of an insider report are to be the form and content required for insider reports under any legislation set out in column 2 of the schedule.
For the purpose of paragraph 273(c) of the Act, the procedure for submission and publication of insider reports is to be the procedure required for the submission and publication of insider reports under any legislation set out in column 2 of the schedule.
A person that is subject to an exemption under any legislation set out in column 2 of the schedule, or an order of the relevant provincial securities regulator, that provides that the person is not an insider for the purposes of the applicable legislation, is not an insider for the purpose of subsection 2(1).
A person that is subject to an exemption from any or all insider reporting requirements under any legislation set out in column 2 of the schedule, or an order of the relevant provincial securities regulator, is also exempt from those requirements for the purposes of these Regulations.
