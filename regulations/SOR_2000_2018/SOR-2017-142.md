# Summary
CUSTOMS TARIFF CUFTA Tariff Preference Regulations.
2017-834 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 16(2) a  of the  Customs Tariff b , makes the annexed  CUFTA Tariff Preference Regulations .
In these Regulations,  originating  means qualifying as originating in the territory of a Party under the rules of origin set out in Chapter Three (Rules of origin and origin procedures) of the Canada–Ukraine Free Trade Agreement.
General.
Coming into Force.
* 3 These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 36 of the  Canada–Ukraine Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act , chapter 8 of the Statutes of Canada, 2017, comes into force, but if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.
[Note: Regulations in force August 1, 2017,  see  SI/2017-37.]
