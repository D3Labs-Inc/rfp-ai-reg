# Title
Name use (Foreign Banks) Regulations


# ID
SOR-2008-156

# URL
http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-2008-156/FullText.html


# Summary
Non-bank affiliate and foreign bank For the purpose of paragraph 983(4)(g) of the Act, the word “bank”, “banker” or “banking” may be used in a description of the corporate relationship between a non-bank affiliate of a foreign bank, within the meaning of subsection 507(1) of the Act, and a foreign bank that controls the non-bank affiliate if the description includes a statement that the foreign bank is not regulated in Canada as a financial institution, a bank holding company or an insurance holding company.
Non-bank affiliate and entity associated with foreign bank For the purpose of paragraph 983(4)(h) of the Act, the word “bank”, “banker” or “banking” may be used in a description of the corporate relationship between a non-bank affiliate of a foreign bank, within the meaning of subsection 507(1) of the Act, and an entity associated with a foreign bank that controls the non-bank affiliate if the description includes a statement that the entity associated with a foreign bank is not regulated in Canada as a financial institution, a bank holding company or an insurance holding company.
