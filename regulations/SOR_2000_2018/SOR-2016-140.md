# Summary
CUSTOMS TARIFF Ferry-Boats Remission Order, 2016.
2016-509 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 115 a  of the  Customs Tariff b , makes the annexed  Ferry-Boats Remission Order, 2016 .
Ferry-boats Remission is granted of the customs duties paid or payable under the  Customs Tariff  in respect of ferry-boats, classified under subheading No. 8901.10 in the List of Tariff Provisions set out in the schedule to the  Customs Tariff , excluding those produced in Canada that have been exported and then subsequently re-imported into Canada.
Conditions The remission is granted on the following conditions: the ferry-boat was permanently imported into Canada for the first time on or after October 1, 2015; a claim for remission is made by the importer to the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness within two years after the date of importation; and the importer files, on request, all evidence that is required by the Canada Border Services Agency to determine eligibility for remission.
Consequential Amendments to the Ferry-Boats, Tankers and Cargo Vessels Remission Order, 2010.
