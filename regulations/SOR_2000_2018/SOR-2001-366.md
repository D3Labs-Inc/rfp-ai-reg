# Title
Ancillary Activities (Insurance Companies, Canadian Societies and Insurance Holding Companies) Regulations


# ID
SOR-2001-366

# URL
http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-2001-366/FullText.html


# Summary
24] Prescribed activities For the purpose of paragraph 495(4)(f) of the Act, the following activities are prescribed in relation to an entity and a property and casualty company or marine company that acquires control of, or acquires or increases a substantial investment in, the entity: the provision of safety and risk prevention services and services respecting risk management and claims adjustment; the operation of rehabilitation and training and development centres; the provision of computer systems to independent insurance brokers and agents; the provision of support to independent insurance brokers and agents; the operation of repair and appraisal centres; and any other activities that are reasonably ancillary to the business of insurance carried on by the company.
Prescribed activities For the purpose of paragraph 971(2)(f) of the Act, the following activities are prescribed in relation to an entity and an insurance holding company that acquires control of, or acquires or increases a substantial investment in, the entity: the provision of safety and risk prevention services and services respecting risk management and claims adjustment; the operation of rehabilitation and training and development centres; the provision of computer systems to independent insurance brokers and agents; the provision of support to independent insurance brokers and agents; the operation of repair and appraisal centres; and any other activities that are reasonably ancillary to the business of insurance carried on by a company controlled by the insurance holding company.
