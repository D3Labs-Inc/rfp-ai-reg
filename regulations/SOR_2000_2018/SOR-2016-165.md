# Summary
CANADA CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY ACT Candles Regulations.
2016-592 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Health, pursuant to section 37 of the  Canada Consumer Product Safety Act a , makes the annexed  Candles Regulations .
21 Requirement.
Spontaneous reignition A candle must not reignite spontaneously after it is extinguished.
Repeal.
[Repeal] Coming into Force.
Registration These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.
