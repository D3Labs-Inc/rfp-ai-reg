# Summary
CUSTOMS TARIFF CPAFTA Rules of Origin Regulations.
2013-309 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 16(2) a  of the  Customs Tariff b , makes the annexed  CPAFTA Rules of Origin Regulations .
36 Rules of Origin.
The following provisions of the Free Trade Agreement between Canada and the Republic of Panama, signed on May 14, 2010, have the force of law in Canada: Articles 3.01 to 3.04; Article 3.05, paragraph 1; Articles 3.06 to 3.15; and Annex 3.02.
Coming into Force.
* 2 These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 38 of the  Canada–Panama Economic Growth and Prosperity Act , chapter 26 of the Statutes of Canada, 2012, comes into force, but if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.
[Note : Regulations in force April 1, 2013,  see  SI/2013-16.]
