# Summary
BANK ACT Aggregate Financial Exposure (Banks) Regulations.
2001-1734 Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 978 a  of the  Bank Act b , hereby makes the annexed  Aggregate Financial Exposure (Banks) Regulations .
Definition of  Act In these Regulations,  Act  means the  Bank Act .
Aggregate Financial Exposure.
Coming into force * 3 These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 495.2 of the  Bank Act , as enacted by section 129 of the  Financial Consumer Agency of Canada Act , chapter 9 of the Statutes of Canada, 2001, comes into force.
[Note: Regulations in force October 24, 2001,  see  SI/2001-102.]
