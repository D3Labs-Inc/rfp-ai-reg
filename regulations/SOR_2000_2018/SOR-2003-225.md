# Summary
AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS MARKETING ACT Saskatchewan Canola Order.
2003-918 Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Agriculture and Agri-Food, pursuant to sectionÂ 2 a  of the  Agricultural Products Marketing Act , hereby makes the annexed  Saskatchewan Canola Order .
Act  means  The Agri-Food Act  of the Province of Saskatchewan.
( Loi ) canola  means canola, rapeseed or oilseed, of the genus  Brassica , that is produced in Saskatchewan.
( colza ) Commission  means the Saskatchewan Canola Development Commission.
( Commission ) Plan  means  The Saskatchewan Canola Development Plan Regulations  made under the Act.
The Commission is authorized to regulate the marketing of canola in interprovincial and export trade and for that purpose may, with respect to persons and property situated within the Province of Saskatchewan, exercise all or any powers like the powers exercisable by it in relation to the marketing of canola locally within the Province under the Act and the Plan.
[Repeal] Coming into Force.
This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
