# Summary
CANADA OIL AND GAS OPERATIONS ACT Regulations Establishing a List of Spill-treating Agents (Canada Oil and Gas Operations Act).
The Minister of the Environment, pursuant to section 14.2 a  of the  Canada Oil and Gas Operations Act b , establishes the annexed  Regulations Establishing a List of Spill-treating Agents (Canada Oil and Gas Operations Act) .
2 Gatineau, May 19, 2016 Catherine McKenna Minister of the Environment La ministre de l’Environnement List.
The list set out in the schedule is established for the purposes of the definition  spill-treating agent  in section 2 of the  Canada Oil and Gas Operations Act .
Coming into force.
These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.
(Section 1) List of Spill-Treating Agents.
