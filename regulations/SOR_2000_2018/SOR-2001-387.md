# Summary
BANK ACT COOPERATIVE CREDIT ASSOCIATIONS ACT INSURANCE COMPANIES ACT TRUST AND LOAN COMPANIES ACT Factoring Entity Regulations.
2001-1758 Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to sections 978 a  of the  Bank Act b , 463 c  of the  Cooperative Credit Associations Act d , 1021 e  of the  Insurance Companies Act f  and 531 g  of the  Trust and Loan Companies Act h , hereby makes the annexed  Factoring Entity Regulations .
Coming into force * 2 These Regulations come into force on the day on which sections 464 and 522.22 of the  Bank Act , 386 of the  Cooperative Credit Associations Act , 490 of the  Insurance Companies Act  and 449 of the  Trust and Loan Companies Act , as enacted by sections 127, 132, 314, 426 and 550, respectively, of the  Financial Consumer Agency of Canada Act , chapter 9 of the Statutes of Canada, 2001, come into force.
