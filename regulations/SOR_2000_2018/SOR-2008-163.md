BANK ACT Material Banking Group Percentage Regulations.
2008-943 Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 978 a  of the  Bank Act b , hereby makes the annexed  Material Banking Group Percentage Regulations .
46 Prescribed Percentage.
Prescribed percentage For the purposes of subsection 508(3) of the  Bank Act , the percentage is 50%.
