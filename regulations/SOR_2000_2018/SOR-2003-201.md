# Summary
EXCISE ACT, 2001 Regulations Respecting the Information to be Displayed on Alcohol Containers and their Packaging.
2003-855 Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to paragraph 304(1)(f) of the  Excise Act, 2001 a , hereby makes the annexed  Regulations Respecting the Information to be Displayed on Alcohol Containers and their Packaging .
22 Information.
For the purpose of section 87 of the  Excise Act, 2001 , the prescribed information to be displayed is the name and address of the alcohol licensee who packaged the alcohol; or their licence number.
Coming into Force.
These Regulations take effect on July 1, 2003.
