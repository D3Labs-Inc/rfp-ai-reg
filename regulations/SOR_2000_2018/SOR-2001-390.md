# Title
Form of Proxy (Banks and Bank Holding Companies) Regulations


# ID
SOR-2001-390

# URL
http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-2001-390/FullText.html


# Summary
Meaning of certain words For the purpose of subsection (1), in the provisions referred to in that subsection, except in respect of subparagraph 35(ee)(i) and paragraph 35(gg) of the  Canada Business Corporations Regulations , a reference to a “corporation” shall be read as a reference to a “bank” or a “bank holding company”, as the case may be; beneficial ownership  and  security  have the same meaning as in section 2 of the Act; control ,  holding body corporate ,  subsidiary  and  affiliate  have the same meaning as in sections 3, 4, 5 and 6, respectively, of the Act; and insider  has the same meaning as in section 265 of the Act, in the case of a bank, and section 265 of the Act, as adapted by section 834 of the Act, in the case of a bank holding company.
Meaning of  Corporation For the purpose of subsection (1), in subparagraph 35(ee)(i) and paragraph 35(gg) of the  Canada Business Corporations Regulations , a reference to a “corporation” shall be read as a reference to a “bank, bank holding company or corporation”.
