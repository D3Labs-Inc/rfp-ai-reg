# Summary
Message Subject to subsection (3), no vessel may enter a shipping safety control zone in the conditions set out in subsection 8(2), unless the master of the vessel sends a message to the Minister, in the manner determined by the Minister, containing the following information: the call letters and type of vessel and the name of any escorting vessel, the name and certification information of the master, and the certification information of any persons referred to in paragraph 12.3.2 of Part I-A of the Polar Code and the person referred to in section 10, if applicable, a description of the proposed route through the zone, a description of each ice regime anticipated on the proposed route, and the ice numeral or the risk index outcome for the regime, and the final destination of the vessel; and has received an acknowledgement of the message from the Minister.
Non-application Subsections (1) to (3) and (5) do not apply to vessels referred to in paragraph 46(2)(b) of the  Vessel Pollution and Dangerous Chemicals Regulations .
