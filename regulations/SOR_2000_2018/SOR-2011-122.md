# Summary
ROYAL CANADIAN MOUNTED POLICE PENSION CONTINUATION ACT FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT Royal Canadian Mounted Police (Dependants) Pension Fund Increase in Benefits Order.
836109 Whereas it appears from a report made under section 56 of the  Royal Canadian Mounted Police Pension Continuation Act a  that the Royal Canadian Mounted Police (Dependants) Pension Fund is substantially in excess of the amount required to make adequate provision for the prospective payments out of it; R.S.C. 1970, c.
R-10 Therefore, the Treasury Board, on the recommendation of the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness, pursuant to subsection 57(1) of the  Royal Canadian Mounted Police Pension Continuation Act a  and paragraph 7(2)(e) of the  Financial Administration Act b , hereby makes the annexed  Royal Canadian Mounted Police (Dependants) Pension Fund Increase in Benefits Order .
F-11 The following pension benefits, as provided in Part IV of the  Royal Canadian Mounted Police Pension Continuation Act  R.S.C. 1970, c.
