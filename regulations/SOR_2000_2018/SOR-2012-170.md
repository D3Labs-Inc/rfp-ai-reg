# Summary
STATE IMMUNITY ACT Order Establishing a List of Foreign State Supporters of Terrorism.
2012-1067 Whereas the Governor in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Foreign Affairs made after consultation with the Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness, is satisfied that there are reasonable grounds to believe that each of the foreign states set out on the list established by the annexed Order supported or supports terrorism; Therefore, His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Foreign Affairs, pursuant to subsection 6.1(2) a  of the  State Immunity Act b , makes the annexed  Order Establishing a List of Foreign State Supporters of Terrorism .
S-18 List The list set out in the schedule is established for the purposes of section 6.1 of the  State Immunity Act .
Coming into force This Order comes into force on September 7, 2012.
(Section 1) Foreign States.
Islamic Republic of Iran Syrian Arab Republic
