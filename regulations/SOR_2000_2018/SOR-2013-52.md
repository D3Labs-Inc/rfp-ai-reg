# Summary
CUSTOMS TARIFF CPAFTA Tariff Preference Regulations.
2013-311 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 16(2) a  of the  Customs Tariff b , makes the annexed  CPAFTA Tariff Preference Regulations .
In these Regulations,  originating  means qualifying as originating in the territory of a Party under the rules of origin set out in Chapter Three (Rules of Origin) of the Canada-Panama Free Trade Agreement, signed on May 14, 2010.
General.
Coming into Force.
* 3 These Regulations come into force on the day on which section 42 of the  Canada–Panama Economic Growth and Prosperity Act , chapter 26 of the Statutes of Canada, 2012, comes into force, but if they are registered after that day, they come into force on the day on which they are registered.
[Note: Regulations in force April 1, 2013,  see  SI/2013-16.]
