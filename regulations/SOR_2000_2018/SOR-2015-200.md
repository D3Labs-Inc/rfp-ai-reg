# Title
General Export Permit No. 41 — Dual-use Goods and Technology to Certain Destinations


# ID
SOR-2015-200

# URL
http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/regulations/SOR-2015-200/FullText.html


# Summary
Authorization Subject to sections 3 to 5, any resident of Canada may, under this Permit, export or transfer from Canada any good or technology referred to in any item in Group 1 of the Guide; or items 5504.2.a. to 5504.2.g. of the Guide.
Other unauthorized goods or technology This Permit does not authorize the export or transfer of software that is specially designed or modified for the development or use of the goods or technology referred to in item 5504.2.h. or 5504.2.i. of the Guide; or technology that is specially designed or modified for the development or production of the goods or technology referred to in item 5504.2.h. or 5504.2.i. of the Guide.
of the Guide that are software specially designed for the development or production of equipment specified by item 1-6.B.8.
of the Guide that are software specially designed for the development or production of equipment specified by item 1-8.A.1.b., 1-8.A.1.d., or 1-8.A.2.o.3.b. of the Guide 39 those referred to in item 1-8.E.1.
of the Guide that are software specially designed or modified for the development of equipment or technology specified by item 1-9.A.11., 1-9.E.3.a.1.
of the Guide that are software specially designed or modified for the production of equipment specified by item 1-9.A.11.
