# Summary
INSURANCE COMPANIES ACT Exemption for Public Notices or Documents (Insurance Companies and Insurance Holding Companies) Regulations.
2010-1333 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 1021(1) a  and section 1047 b  of the  Insurance Companies Act c , hereby makes the annexed  Exemption for Public Notices or Documents Regulations (Insurance Companies and Insurance Holding Companies) Regulations .
47 Interpretation.
In these Regulations,  Act  means the  Insurance Companies Act .
Exemption Circumstances.
For the purpose of section 1047 of the Act, the prescribed circumstances are that the exemption does not prejudice any of the shareholders or policyholders entitled to vote or the public interest.
Coming into Force.
These Regulations come into force on June 1, 2011.
