# Summary
Single small-scale facility or other facility Any person that anticipates carrying out, during the following calendar year, an activity at the single small-scale facility or an other facility under an authorization referred to in subsection 8(1) of the Act must make, no later than September 15 of the current year, an annual declaration of the activities that are anticipated to be carried out during the calendar year to the National Authority that is signed and dated by the declarant or, in the case of a corporation, by its authorized representative and that contains the following information: the name and contact information of the declarant; if applicable, the name, position and contact information of the authorized representative; the name of the facility and its address or, if there is no address, a description of its precise location; for each Schedule 1 chemical referred to in the declaration, its IUPAC name and CAS registry number or, if no CAS registry number has been assigned, its structural formula, and the quantity that is anticipated to be produced and the purposes for which it will be produced and, in the case of an other facility, the calendar yearly quarter during which the production is anticipated to take place; and any changes that are anticipated at the facility compared to, in the case of the single small-scale facility, its detailed technical description, including the inventory of equipment and detailed diagrams, and in the case of an other facility, its detailed technical description.

| 1996-01-01 | Declaration Unless the information has already been submitted to the National Authority, any person that, during a calendar year that falls within the period beginning on January 1, 1996 and ending at the end of the calendar year before the coming into force of these Regulations, produced by synthesis a discrete organic chemical at a plant site must make, within 90 days after the day on which these Regulations come into force, an initial declaration to the National Authority that is signed and dated by the declarant or, in the case of a corporation, by its authorized representative and that contains, for each calendar year referred to in this section during which such production took place, the following information: the name and contact information of the declarant; if applicable, the name, position and contact information of the authorized representative; for each plant site, its name, address and precise location, the name of its owner or of the corporation or enterprise operating it, as the case may be, the main activities that were carried out, and the number of plants within the plant site where a discrete organic chemical was produced by synthesis; the approximate aggregate quantity of discrete organic chemicals that were produced by synthesis during the calendar year, expressed in one of the following ranges: more than 200 tonnes but less than 1 000 tonnes, 1 000 tonnes or more but less than or equal to 10 000 tonnes, or more than 10 000 tonnes; and if a PSF chemical was produced by synthesis, the number of plants within the plant site where a PSF chemical was produced by synthesis, and for each of those plants, the approximate aggregate quantity of PSF chemicals that were produced by synthesis there during the calendar year, expressed in one of the following ranges: more than 30 tonnes but less than 200 tonnes, 200 tonnes or more but less than 1 000 tonnes, 1 000 tonnes or more but less than or equal to 10 000 tonnes, or more than 10 000 tonnes.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
