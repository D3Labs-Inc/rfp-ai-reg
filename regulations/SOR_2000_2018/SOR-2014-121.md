# Summary
SOFTWOOD LUMBER PRODUCTS EXPORT CHARGE ACT, 2006 Expiry of Section 12.2 of the Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006 Regulations.
2014-577 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of National Revenue, pursuant to paragraph 101(a) and section 102 of the  Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006 a , makes the annexed  Expiry of Section 12.2 of the Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006 Regulations .
13 Ceasing to be in force Section 12.2 of the  Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Act, 2006  ceases to be in force on October 12, 2013.
Coming into force These Regulations are deemed to have come into force on October 13, 2013.
