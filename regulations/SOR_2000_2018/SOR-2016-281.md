# Summary
SPECIES AT RISK ACT Critical Habitat of the Roseate Tern (Sterna dougallii) Order.
29 Whereas the recovery strategy that identified the critical habitat of that species has been included in the Species at Risk Public Registry; Whereas a portion of the critical habitat of that species is in a place referred to in subsection 58(2) b  of that Act and, under subsection 58(5) of that Act, that portion must be excluded from the annexed Order; S.C. 2015, c.
Subsection 58(1) of the  Species at Risk Act  applies to the portion of the critical habitat of the Roseate Tern ( Sterna dougallii ) — which critical habitat is identified in the recovery strategy for that species that is included in the Species at Risk Public Registry — that is located within the following areas: Country Island in Nova Scotia and an area extending 200 m seaward from the mean high tide line; an area extending 200 m seaward from the mean high tide line of each of North Brother Island and South Brother Island in Nova Scotia; and any area that is within a 200 m perimeter of each polygon encompassing each individual nesting colony of any species of tern on Sable Island in Nova Scotia, excluding any area that is within the Sable Island National Park Reserve of Canada, as described in Schedule 2 of the  Canada National Parks Act .
