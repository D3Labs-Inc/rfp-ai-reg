# Summary
INSURANCE COMPANIES ACT Subsidiaries that Hold Insurance Holding Company Shares Regulations.
2001-1805 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 1021 a  of the  Insurance Companies Act b , hereby makes the annexed  Subsidiaries that Hold Insurance Holding Company Shares Regulations .
distribution mise en circulation distribution  means a trade by or on behalf of an insurance holding company in securities of the insurance holding company that have not previously been issued; or a trade in previously issued securities of an insurance holding company from the holdings of any person or group of persons who act in concert and who hold in excess of 10% of the shares of any class of voting shares of the insurance holding company.
Holding of Insurance Holding Company’s Shares by Subsidiary.
Exception for securities underwriter If a subsidiary of an insurance holding company is a regulated securities entity and is acting as a securities underwriter in connection with a distribution of shares of the insurance holding company, the insurance holding company may permit the subsidiary to hold shares of the insurance holding company in excess of the limit set out in section 2 until the distribution is ended.
