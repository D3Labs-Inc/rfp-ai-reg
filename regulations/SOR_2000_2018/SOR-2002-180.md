# Summary
CANADIAN AIR TRANSPORT SECURITY AUTHORITY ACT CATSA Aerodrome Designation Regulations.
2002-737 Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Transport, pursuant to paragraph 34(a) of the  Canadian Air Transport Security Authority Act a , hereby makes the annexed  CATSA Aerodrome Designation Regulations .
The aerodromes set out in the schedule are designated for the purposes of the  Canadian Air Transport Security Authority Act .
