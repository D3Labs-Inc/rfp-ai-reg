# Summary
EXPORT AND IMPORT PERMITS ACT Exemption Regulations (Persons).
2008-403 Her Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Foreign Affairs, pursuant to paragraph 12(e) a  of the  Export and Import Permits Act , hereby makes the annexed  Exemption Regulations (Persons) .
The following persons are exempt from the provisions of the  Export and Import Permits Act  while acting in the course of their employment: employees of a foreign state who, for the purpose of ensuring public safety or security, carry goods in the course of their employment when entering or exiting Canada for which permits are required under the  Export and Import Permits Act  and whose duties and functions are described in an arrangement or commitment between the foreign state and the Government of Canada; members of the Correctional Service of Canada who are designated as peace officers under section 10 of the  Corrections and Conditional Release Act ; police officers or police constables who are members of a police force in Canada; persons designated as fishery officers under the  Fisheries Act  when performing any duties or functions under that Act or the  Coastal Fisheries Protection Act ; employees of the Canadian Coast Guard; persons designated under section 6 of the  Migratory Birds Convention Act, 1994 , section 11 of the  Canada Wildlife Act , section 12 of the  Wild Animal and Plant Protection and Regulation of International and Interprovincial Trade Act  or section 85 of the  Species at Risk Act ; and persons referred to in paragraph (d) or (d.1) of the definition  peace officer  in section 2 of the  Criminal Code  and persons referred to in paragraph (b) or (f) of the definition  public officer  in subsection 117.07(2) of that Act. SOR/2009-29, s.
These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.
