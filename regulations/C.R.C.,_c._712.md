# Summary
FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT Order Pursuant to Section 13 of the Financial Administration Act Authorizing the Minister of Agriculture to Prescribe Certain Fees.
Short Title.
This Order may be cited as the  Minister of Agriculture Authority to Prescribe Fees Order .
Authorization.
The Minister of Agriculture is authorized to prescribe by order the fees to be charged for services provided by Her Majesty with respect to the inspection of milk production of dairy cattle and dairy goats in Canada; and the performance testing of beef cattle, sheep and swine in Canada.
