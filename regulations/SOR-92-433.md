# Summary
Where at any time the Chief Electoral Officer requests an assistant returning officer to carry out a special assignment, the assistant returning officer shall be paid a fee for all services performed in connection with that assignment, per hour, of  $Â 20.75 for travel and living expenses in connection with the services referred to in paragraph (a)  the allowances and expenses referred to in item 33 Information Sessions on Referendum Matters.
