# Summary
FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT Regulations Prescribing the Fee To Be Paid for the Verification of a Criminal Record for Civil Purposes.
1993-1822  His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Solicitor General of Canada and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(a) *  of the  Financial Administration Act , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Regulations prescribing the fee to be paid for the verification of a criminal record for civil purposes .
6 Short Title.
These Regulations may be cited as the  Royal Canadian Mounted Police, Criminal Record Verification for Civil Purposes Fee Regulations .
Fee. The fee to be paid by an individual for the verification by the Royal Canadian Mounted Police of a criminal record for civil purposes is $25.
