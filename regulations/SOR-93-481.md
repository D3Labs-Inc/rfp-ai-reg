# Summary
FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT Regulations Prescribing the Fee for Fingerprinting for the Purposes of a Visa, Licence or Security Clearance.
1993-1818  His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Solicitor General of Canada and the Treasury Board, pursuant to paragraph 19(1)(a) *  of the  Financial Administration Act , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Regulations prescribing the fee for fingerprinting for the purposes of a visa, licence or security clearance .
6 Short Title.
These Regulations may be cited as the  Royal Canadian Mounted Police, Fingerprinting for Visa, Licensing or Security Clearance Purposes Fee Regulations .
Fee. The fee to be paid by an individual for fingerprinting for the purposes of obtaining a visa, licence or security clearance is $25.
