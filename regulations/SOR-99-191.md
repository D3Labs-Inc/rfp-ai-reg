# Summary
STATUS OF THE ARTIST ACT Status of the Artist Act Professional Category Regulations.
1999-736  His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Labour after consultation with the Minister of Canadian Heritage, pursuant to subparagraph 6(2)(b)(iii) and section 56 a  of the  Status of the Artist Act b , hereby makes the annexed  Status of the Artist Act Professional Category Regulations .
The professional categories prescribed by subsection (1) do not include any profession in which the practitioner of the profession carries out, in connection with an activity referred to in subsection (1), the activities of accounting, auditing, legal, representation, publicity or management work or clerical, administrative or other support work; or is a person referred to in subparagraph 6(2)(b)(i) of the Act or carries out an activity referred to in subparagraph 6(2)(b)(ii) of the Act. Coming into Force.
These Regulations come into force on April 22, 1999.
