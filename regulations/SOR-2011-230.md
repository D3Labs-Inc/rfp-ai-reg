# Summary
INTEREST ACT Prescribed Entities and Classes of Mortgages and Hypothecs Regulations.
2011-1235 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 10(3) a  of the  Interest Act b , hereby makes the annexed  Prescribed Entities and Classes of Mortgages and Hypothecs Regulations .
I-15 Prescribed entities, mortgages and hypothecs For the purposes of paragraph 10(2)(b) of the  Interest Act , the following entities are prescribed: partnerships, trusts settled for business or commercial purposes, unlimited liability corporations as defined in the  Business Corporations Act , R.S.A. 2000, c.
B-9, unlimited liability companies as defined in the  Business Corporations Act , S.B.C. 2002, c.
57, and unlimited companies as defined in the  Companies Act , R.S.N.S. 1989, c.
81; and the prescribed class of mortgages and hypothecs consists of those issued after January 1, 2012.
Coming into force These Regulations come into force on January 1, 2012.
