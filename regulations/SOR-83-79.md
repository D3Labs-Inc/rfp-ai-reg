# Summary
CURRENCY ACT Regulations Respecting Conversion of Values Expressed in Gold Francs into Canadian Dollar Equivalents for Purposes of Subsection 2(6) of the Carriage by Air Act. 1983-19 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to section 13.1 of the  Currency and Exchange Act , is pleased hereby to make the annexed Regulations respecting conversion of values expressed in gold francs into Canadian dollar equivalents for purposes of subsection 2(6) of the  Carriage by Air Act .
These Regulations may be cited as the  Carriage by Air Act Gold Franc Conversion Regulations .
In these Regulations, gold francs  means the francs referred to in subsection 2(6) of the  Carriage by Air Act ; ( francs-or ) S.D.R.
means the special drawing rights issued by the International Monetary Fund.
For the purposes of subsection 2(6) of the  Carriage by Air Act , the equivalent dollar value of gold francs shall be determined as follows: gold francs shall be converted into S.D.R.'s at the exchange rate of 15.075 gold francs per S.D.R.; and S.D.R.'s shall be converted into Canadian dollars at the exchange rate established by the International Monetary Fund for S.D.R.'s and Canadian dollars.
