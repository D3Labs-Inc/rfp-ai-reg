# Summary
PETRO-CANADA PUBLIC PARTICIPATION ACT Regulations Respecting Pension Protection for Employees of Petro-Canada Limited.
1991-2406  His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of State (Finance and Privatization) and the President of the Treasury Board, pursuant to section 17 of the  Petro-Canada Public Participation Act * , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Regulations respecting pension protection for employees of Petro-Canada Limited .
The  Public Service Superannuation Act  and the  Supplementary Retirement Benefits Act  and the regulations made under those Acts apply to a person who makes an election under paragraph 17(1)(d) of the  Petro-Canada Limited Public Participation Act  except as provided in sections 3 and 4.
For the purposes of section 17 of the  Petro-Canada Limited Public Participation Act , subsection 4(5) and subparagraphs 6(2)(b)(i) and (ii) of the  Supplementary Retirement Benefits Act  are adapted as follows: in subsection 4(5) of that Act, the retirement year or retirement month of a person is 1991 or July 1991, as the case may be; and in subparagraphs 6(2)(b)(i) and (ii) of that Act, the year in which a person ceased to contribute in respect of current service to the Supplementary Retirement Benefits Account is the year in which that person ceased to be employed by Petro-Canada Limited.
