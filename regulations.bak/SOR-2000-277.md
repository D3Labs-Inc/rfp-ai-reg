# Summary
CANADA DEPOSIT INSURANCE CORPORATION ACT Deposit Insurance Application Fee By-law.
The Board of Directors of the Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation, pursuant to paragraph 11(2)(g) a  and subsection 18(1) b  of the  Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation Act , hereby makes the annexed  Deposit Insurance Application Fee By-law .
June 29, 2000 R.S. c.
18 (3rd Supp.), s.
100 Prescribed Fee. The prescribed fee that is to accompany an application for deposit insurance made by a provincial institution under subsection 17(1.1) of the  Canada Deposit Insurance Corporation Act  is $20,000 and is non-refundable.
SOR/2006-235, s.
1. Coming into Force.
This By-law comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
