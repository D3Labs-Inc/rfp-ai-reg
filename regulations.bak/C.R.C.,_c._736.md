# Summary
FINANCIAL ADMINISTRATION ACT Order Authorizing the Minister of National Defence to Transfer Academic Hoods to Recipients of Honorary Degrees.
Short Title.
This Order may be cited as the  Transfer of Royal Military College Academic Hoods Order .
Authorization.
The Minister of National Defence is authorized to transfer academic hoods from time to time to recipients of honorary degrees conferred by the Royal Military College of Canada, Kingston, Ontario.
