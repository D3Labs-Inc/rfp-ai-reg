# Summary
INDIAN ACT Sandy Bay Band Council Elections Order.
Whereas the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development deems it advisable for the good government of the Sandy Bay Band that the council of the Band, consisting of a chief and councillors, shall, beginning on February 26, 2003, be selected by elections to be held in accordance with the  Indian Act ; Therefore, the Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development, pursuant to subsection 74(1) of the  Indian Act , hereby makes the annexed  Sandy Bay Band Council Elections Order .
Gatineau, Quebec, February 25, 2003 Robert D.
Nault Minister of Indian Affairs and Northern Development Elections.
The council of the Sandy Bay Band, consisting of a chief and councillors, shall, beginning on February 26, 2003, be selected by elections to be held in accordance with the  Indian Act .
Coming into Force.
This Order comes into force on the day on which it is registered.
