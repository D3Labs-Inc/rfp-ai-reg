# Summary
CANADA CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY ACT Infant Feeding Bottle Nipples Regulations.
2016-607 His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Health, pursuant to section 37 of the  Canada Consumer Product Safety Act a , makes the annexed  Infant Feeding Bottle Nipples Regulations .
21 Interpretation.
Definition of  infant feeding bottle nipple In these Regulations,  infant feeding bottle nipple  includes a similar product that is put in the mouth of an infant during use.
Requirement.
Limit of volatile N-nitrosamines An infant feeding bottle nipple must not contain more than 10 µg/kg total volatile N-nitrosamines, as determined by dichloromethane extraction.
Repeal.
[Repeal] Coming into Force.
Registration These Regulations come into force on the day on which they are registered.
