# Summary
EXCISE TAX ACT Regulations Prescribing Taxable Goods Imported by Mail or Courier.
1990-2741  His Excellency the Governor General in Council, on the recommendation of the Minister of Finance, pursuant to subsection 277(1) *  of the  Excise Tax Act , is pleased hereby to make the annexed  Regulations prescribing taxable goods imported by mail or courier .
These Regulations may be cited as the  Mail and Courier Imports (GST/HST) Regulations .
In these Regulations,  Act  means the  Excise Tax Act .
( Loi ) Prescribed Goods.
For the purposes of section 7 of Schedule VII to the Act, the following imported goods are prescribed goods: excisable goods and wine; goods that are prescribed property for the purposes of section 143.1 of the Act, if the supplier of the goods is required to register under Subdivision d of Division V of Part IX of the Act and is not so registered; goods for which the value for duty is reduced by the application of section 85 of the  Customs Tariff ; goods that are purchased from a retailer in Canada and mailed or transported from outside Canada directly to the purchaser; and goods that are purchased or ordered through or from a person in Canada acting for or on behalf of a person outside Canada who is selling the goods.
Subsection 2(1) applies to goods imported after November 26, 1997.
